About Me

I didn’t start as a virtual assistant. I started by managing real responsibilities. Handling finances, organizing records, and keeping day-to-day operations running without things falling apart.

In a homeowners association, I handled bookkeeping. I tracked income and expenses and made sure records stayed accurate. In a yoga studio, I managed the front desk. Scheduling, client support, and daily admin tasks were part of my routine.

That experience shaped how I work. I stay organized. I pay attention to details. I follow through. I don’t let tasks sit or get lost.

Now I support business owners who need structure. If your inbox is piling up, your leads are not tracked, or your schedule feels messy, I step in and fix that.

You focus on growing your business. I make sure everything behind the scenes runs smoothly.
